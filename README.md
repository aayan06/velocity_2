# Velocity 2

An endless auto-runner platformer built with Phaser 3.

## How to Play
- **D** - Dash (5 second cooldown)
- **W / Space** - Jump
- **R** - Restart after game over

## About
Roll through an endless neon landscape dodging spikes, trees, and ghosts while collecting orbs for a powerful super jump. The game speeds up the longer you survive. Use your dash ability to blast through tight spots and time your jumps carefully to avoid obstacles.

## Features
- Auto-scrolling endless runner with increasing speed
- Dash ability with cooldown timer
- Super jump power up from collecting orbs
- Ghost enemies that patrol and chase when you get close
- Parallax scrolling background with mountains and clouds
- Multiple particle effects for jumping, dashing, landing, and death
- Score tracking with high score
- Background music and sound effects
- Custom character with rolling, jumping, falling, and death animations
- Seamless level looping
